👑 Your Identity in Christ

One of the most important truths a believer can ever discover is this: Your identity is not in your past, your mistakes, your talents, or what others say — your identity is in Christ.

2 Corinthians 5:17 says, “Therefore, if anyone is in Christ, he is a new creation. The old has passed away; behold, the new has come.”

When you gave your life to Jesus, something powerful happened. You were spiritually reborn. You were adopted into God's family. You became royalty, a child of the King!

Here’s who you are in Christ:

  • ✅ You are loved (Romans 8:38-39)
  • ✅ You are chosen (1 Peter 2:9)
  • ✅ You are redeemed (Ephesians 1:7)
  • ✅ You are more than a conqueror (Romans 8:37)
  • ✅ You are seated with Christ in heavenly places (Ephesians 2:6)

The enemy wants to confuse your identity and make you feel like you’re not enough — but God’s Word says you are complete in Christ (Colossians 2:10).

Don’t let the world define you. Let your Creator define you. Walk boldly in who you are, not who you used to be.

You are not what you did — you are what Jesus did for you.

Live like it. Think like it. Speak like it. You belong to Christ! 👑
