🔥 The Call of Salvation: Come Just As You Are

“Come unto me, all ye that labour and are heavy laden, and I will give you rest.” — Matthew 11:28

Salvation is not about being perfect. It’s not about fixing yourself before coming to God. It’s about accepting the free gift Jesus Christ offers through His death and resurrection.

Right now, wherever you are, the call is being made: Come to Jesus. Not tomorrow. Not next week. Now. The blood of Jesus is enough to wash away every sin — no matter how dark your past has been.

He knows your pain. He sees your tears. He understands your struggle. And yet, His arms are still open wide. That’s grace. That’s love. That’s the power of the cross.

There’s a place for you in the Kingdom. Don’t let guilt hold you back. Don’t let shame silence your response. Jesus died for you — and today He says, “Follow Me.”

Will you answer His call?

Say this from your heart:

Lord Jesus, I believe You died for me and rose again. I confess my sins and ask You to come into my heart. Be my Savior, my Lord, and my God. I receive Your gift of salvation. Amen.

If you said that prayer, welcome to the family of God! Get a Bible, start reading the Word, and connect with a local church or Christian group near you.
